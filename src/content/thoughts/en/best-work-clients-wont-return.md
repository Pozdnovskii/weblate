Sounds like bad business, but it's actually what clients need.

For example, a client asked me to create a CTA section for their seasonal sale on their Shopify store. What did I do?

I developed a flexible, reusable section that can be used as a CTA banner or page header. I built it with Liquid, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Store admins have full control through the theme editor — no code changes needed.

They can update content, adjust styles, and choose semantic tags for SEO. They can also control how videos load. The section adapts to different needs while maintaining performance and accessibility.
